Frequently asked questions

How does the custom quoting process work?

It’s easy! Just tell us what you need by filling out this simple form [link to form], and we’ll send you a quote.
Most quotes are returned to you in 1-2 business days.

Can I test your custom service, before I order?

Yes. Your project quote can include a smaller “pilot” batch of cells for you to test in your application. We will ship and bill you for only the pilot batch, at first. If you like the way your cells perform, let us know, and you can then receive the remainder of your custom order.

What is a “pilot batch” of cells?

A pilot batch is a test batch of cells that can be included in your project quote. We highly recommend using pilot batches for new projects, as they allow you to test your competent cells in your application, prior to ordering and paying for the bulk of your order.

How fast will I get my custom cells?

For custom orders of Lucigen strains, you’ll get your cells in 2-3 weeks. For customer-provided strains, turnaround is 4-6 weeks depending on project complexity.

How will my cells be tested?

Every lot of cells is tested for transformation efficiency. We use supercoiled pUC19 plasmid DNA to determine efficiency, or a standard kanamycin-resistant plasmid in cases where the strain genotype indicates resistance to ampicillin. For customer-provided strains, we can use our standard plasmids or a customer-provided plasmid to determine efficiency. Each lot of cells is also tested for resistance to a panel of antibiotics by plating mock-transformed cells on antibiotic plates. Common antibiotic panels include: kanamycin, carbenicillin (ampicillin), streptomycin, tetracycline, gentamycin and chloramphenicol. Lucigen strains must pass strict QC specifications for growth/no growth on appropriate antibiotics.

How will my cells be shipped?

Competent cells must be shipped and stored frozen. Your competent cells will always be shipped on dry ice, and are delivered overnight in the continental US. We also ship internationally through our global network of distributors, as well as direct. Contact Customer Service to learn more.

Can you deliver my cells to me in plates?

Yes. We can use any 96-well plate that you specify. If you’re not sure what you’d like to use, we have some standard suggestions. We can also customize your dispense volume, so you can get the right volume of cells for your application.

Who can I talk to, if I have questions about my order?

Our Customer Service team can help with ordering and delivery questions, and our Technical Support team can help you with any technical questions about the cell strains, or how to use them.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We have a global network of distributors, and we can ship direct in many locations. Contact Customer Service to learn about options in your region.
